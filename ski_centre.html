Ski and Snowboard Centre

At Glasgow Ski and Snowboard Centre learn to ski, learn to board, and learn to do either better! Whether an absolute beginner or an aspiring carver, you'll learn more quickly and have more fun in group lessons. If you are a beginner their 3 magic carpet uplifts will take you up the slopes with the least amount of effort on your part, and for the more experienced they have the Poma Tows.

Open tubing sessions also available on schedule, where the kids can zip down the hill on an inflatable ring.

Facilities
  • Large ski slope
  • Lessons for young children
  • Any skill level
  • Can be used in any weather
  • Good location
